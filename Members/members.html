Christopher Dodson (he/him/his)
Assistant Professor of Engineering (Research)
christopher_dodson@brown.edu
Google Scholar

Chris joined Brown as a research professor in January 2021. His research interests concern the systematic discovery of solid state quantum computing platforms and low-cost sensing methodologies. These disparate themes are united under the goal of efficient and large scale computations and sensing networks. He received a BS in engineering physics from the University of Colorado-Boulder where he did research at NIST. He then earned a PhD in engineering from Brown University, completed postdoctoral work at the University of Washington, and spent five years developing optical sensors and display metrology tooling at Apple before returning to Brown.
